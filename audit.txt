# Audit V9: Final Design Consistency & Orchestration

## 1. Core Logic Stability
- [Sigmoidal Continuity] FIXED. Yellow (#ffff00) and other high-chroma colors now have perfectly smooth ramps thanks to continuous sigmoid falloff in `algo.standard.js`.
- [Gamut Precision] `maxChromaForL` now uses adaptive iterations (up to 32) for Yellow/Cyan boundary zones.
- [CAM16 Stability] Improved post-adaptation power functions prevent low-light rounding errors (0-190 steps).

## 2. Orchestrated Layering
- [Absolute Dark Mode] Dark Mode damping is now the final operation in the pipeline. It correctly tones down Neon, Pastel, and other boosts for visual comfort.
- [Light Mode Boost] New corrective layer for yellow shades, shifting hue away from "Olive Mud".
- [Spectral Balance] Verified Helmholtz–Kohlrausch correction logic.

## 3. UI Views & Experience
- [New Views] Glassmorphism (4-state simulation) and Gradients are fully functional.
- [Fixed Scale Granularity] FIXED. 50/100 granularity now works for Asymmetric and Fixed modes by using `displayStep` for threshold matching.
- [Badges] Step badges (100, 50, BASE) are robust against floating point offsets and always show the closest logical threshold.

## 4. Interaction
- [Chroma Shaping] Suwak od Chroma Shaping jest teraz w pełni zintegrowany z algorytmem sigmoid.
- [Figma Export] Verified SVG generation for new V9 logic.
