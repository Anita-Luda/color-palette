# Audit V5: Design-First Philosophy

## 1. Core Logic Overhaul
- [Asymmetric Scale] FIXED. Correctly generates equal step counts above and below anchor, centering the base color in the distribution.
- [Adaptive Engine] REBUILT. Switched to piece-wise power curves for lightness to guarantee anchor point intersection. Chroma now uses sine-based smooth falloff to ensure "velvet" transitions and stability at extremes.
- [Stability] Eliminated math artifacts (NaNs, extreme spikes) by prioritizing perceptual smoothness over raw gamut maximization where they conflicted.

## 2. Visual Polish
- [Boosts] ALL REWRITTEN.
    - Neon: Pushes toward 90% gamut capacity with controlled headroom.
    - Pastel: Proper high-key compression (top 30% L) and fresh low-chroma targets.
    - Dark Mode: Subtler, more effective hue compensation for UI legibility.
    - Spectral: Balanced perceived brightness without "gray-out" artifacts.
- [Clipping Dots] REFINED. Switched from yellow to grayscale (adaptive contrast). Now purely informative and non-distracting.

## 3. Interaction
- [Clipping Info] Added help button explaining Gamut Clipping logic.
- [Harmony] Relations remain stable across all distribution modes.
